SC.02Director's Statement

"I never picked a lane so made myself a creative bridge between marketing & content"

About Davide

Most people pick a lane. I made a bridge between them. By day I think in funnels, positioning and audience psychology. By night often by day too I'm behind a camera, chasing the shot that makes a scroll stop.

Somewhere between the strategy and the storyboard, I found the job that actually fits: building brands worth watching, then proving it with the numbers behind them. Strategy gives the work a reason to exist; the camera gives it a reason to be remembered.

I run the full chain myself when it matters: brief, shoot, edit, grade, launch, measure. So nothing gets lost in translation between the plan and the frame.
